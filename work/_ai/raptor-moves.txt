Let X be the number of ordinary (non-enter) moves that
the raptors get to make on a single turn.

Then P(X = 0) = 1/11, while for x > 0,

	P(X = x) = sqrt(12/5) * (a^(x+1) - b^(x+1)),

	a = (2 + sqrt(15)) / 11,

	P(X = x) = 4/11 * P(X = x-1) + 1/11 * P(X = x-2)

	P(X = 1) =  48/121,

	P(X ‚â§ x) = 1 -

	P(X ‚â• x) =
